JamNotes

Setlist tracking for live concerts

A tool for the obsessed live music aficionado, JamNotes provides a clean, simple interface for tracking setlists from your favorite live shows. Get the app

App features

Clean UI

JamNotes tries to stay out of your way so you can quickly write down a setlist as it is performed. Not sure how to do something? Just try it, your natural iOS instincts are probably correct.

Learns Your Favorite Acts

JamNotes remembers the names of all the bands, venues, and songs you type. The more you use the app, the faster you can create a new setlist.

Setlist Sharing

Sharing your favorite setlists with your friends on Facebook, Twitter, or by email.

Fast and Efficient

CoreData backend is forward-thinking and scales to millions of records.
